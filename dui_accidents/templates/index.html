Down the Road

A tool for predicting danger hotspots of drunk driving accidents

Fatal Drunk Driving Accidents in California

Remembering past accidents and predicting future accidents

Down the Road is an interactive predictive tool for drunk driving accidents in the state of California. Down the Road was designed to help users better understand past accidents by region and predict future danger hotspots of drunk driving accidents on a given day.

How to use:
- Start by selecting a future date in the format Month Day.
- Counties are then colored by the predicted probability of a fatal drunk driving accident happening on that given day. This allows you to see relative danger levels by county â€“ darker counties have a higher likelihood of an accident.
- Hover over counties to learn more about past accidents on historical days. Better understand the demographic breakdown of the drivers responsible for these accidents on a particular day.
